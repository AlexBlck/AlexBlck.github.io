Bio

I'm a Research Scientist at the AI Theory group at Huawei Research UK. I received my PhD from University of Surrey in 2024 under the supervision of Prof John Collomosse. A year prior I got an MSc in Computer Vision and Machine Learning with Distinction at the same university. I got my BSc in Physics from University of Birmingham. Throughout my graduate studies, I worked in a part-time capacity as a Computer Vision Engineer at two tech startups and did a 6 months internship at Adobe Research.
